Which Teleprompter Is Right For Your Production Scenario?

1. Straight-to-Camera Speech

Recommended: Standard Through-the-Lens Teleprompter. Ideal for traditional addresses, direct corporate communications, and direct-to-lens speaking rules.

2. Live Events & Corporate Keynotes

Recommended: Presidential Teleprompter systems or Downstage Monitors (DSMs).

• Presidential Prompter: Eliminates the stress when a CEO or politician speaks to an audience for either a live or prerecorded corporate event. (Ask about our ultra-premium Motorized Robotic Rise & Fall systems).

• Downstage Monitors: Features one or more discreet, low-profile prompter monitors placed precisely at the edge of the stage floor.

3. Documentary & Corporate Interviews

Recommended: The Interrotron System.

By utilizing a specialized two-way mirror mechanism, the Interrotron allows the interviewee to maintain direct eye contact with the filmmaker or interviewer while looking straight into the camera lens. This immediately relaxes the subject, removes camera anxiety, and draws out a highly authentic, honest, and natural emotional response much faster than a standard setup.

Explore our specialized Interrotron Page →

4. Cameras on a Jib or Dolly

Recommended: Wireless Jib Prompter. A highly lightweight, professional through-the-lens system engineered to slide directly onto the existing rod architecture of your camera configuration without disrupting balance or crane movement.

5. Handheld Shoots & Steadicam Rigs

Recommended: Steadicam Prompter. An ultra-lightweight, compact, through-the-lens prompting build meticulously configured not to overload or throw off the balance calibrations of complex stabilization setups.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can the audience see the script?

No. The script is visible to the speaker via specialized optical beam-splitter glass, completely transparent to the audience.

Do I need a teleprompter operator?

For most professional shoots and live events, yes. An operator keeps the script moving naturally, dynamically matching the speaker's scrolling pace.

Can you make script changes during the shoot?

Yes. Script changes can be made on set instantly by our technical operator so your speaker can continue smoothly.
