Frequently Asked Fleet Questions

What happens if a script is modified live on set?

Our dedicated operators manipulate script layouts via industry-standard prompting engines. They process text variations, shift run orders, and handle font layouts immediately upon request without disrupting the talent.

Are your monitoring systems compatible with all cinema cameras?

Yes. Our sled systems accommodate everything from compact mirrorless form factors up to oversized studio structures using full baseplate standard rails. We take power via standard inputs and accept direct SDI or HDMI feeds.

How do your multi-day booking discounts scale?

Our system integrates a multi-day discount curve directly into structural estimates. Bookings running 3-4 days receive a 10% daily rate reduction, and configurations running 5 days or longer scale to a 15% rate discount automatically calculated on checkout.
